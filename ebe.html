Admissions

The University Of Northernham admits students from Europe, Africa and throughout the whole world,representing different perspectives,experiences, backgrounds and cultures. The university through the Office of International Programmes encourages participation of international students through direct enrollment and exchange programmes. At present, international students number close to 5,000 students, drawn from over 75 countries.

Undergraduate Admission

The University of Northernham annouces to the general public that applications are open for the admission of prospective into the various undergraduate programmes for the 2022/2023 academic year. The university offers variuous undergraduate programmes such as; 1. Doctor of Veterinary Medicine 2. Computer Science 3. Engineering programmes such as; Biomedical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Telecommunication Engineering etc.
